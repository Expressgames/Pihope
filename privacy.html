Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 22, 2025

PiHope ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect basic information voluntarily provided by users who are in need. This includes:

  • Name
  • Donation request description (such as purpose, need, etc.)

We do not collect sensitive personal data, nor do we track users who only wish to donate.

2. How We Use the Information

The information is used solely to display genuine requests for donations. It is not sold, shared, or used for any other purpose.

3. Data Storage

Your data is securely stored in our cloud-based database and only accessible by our internal system. No third party has access to this information.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies or any tracking technologies in the PiHope app.

5. Third-Party Services

We do not share data with third-party vendors or services.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please email us at pihope.in@gmail.com.
